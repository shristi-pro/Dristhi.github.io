About Us

Building Nepal's Future Through Engineering Excellence

Dristhi Engineering & Construction Pvt. Ltd. is a multidisciplinary engineering consultancy, construction, interior design, and infrastructure development company based in Nepal.

We provide comprehensive engineering, architectural, construction, renovation, and project management services for residential, commercial, industrial, and government projects.

Driven by innovation and technical excellence, we deliver practical, sustainable, and cost-effective solutions tailored to each client's vision and requirements. From planning and design to construction, renovation, interior finishing, and maintenance, we ensure every project meets the highest standards of quality, safety, and durability.

Our experienced team of engineers, architects, project managers, surveyors, designers, and skilled professionals is committed to transforming ideas into reality through modern technology, professional expertise, and exceptional customer service.
